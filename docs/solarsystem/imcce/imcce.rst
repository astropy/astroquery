***********************************************
IMCCE Services (`astroquery.solarsystem.imcce`)
***********************************************

This submodule is a collection of Solar System-related services
provided by the `Institut de Mécanique Céleste et de Calcul des Éphémérides (IMCCE)
<https://www.imcce.fr/>`_. Modules, classes, and functions in this
submodule can be imported as part of this submodule or directly from
their top-level modules.

  :maxdepth: 1

Future Additions
================

An interface to IMCCE's `Miriade
<http://vo.imcce.fr/webservices/miriade/>`_ service will be added to
this submodule in the future.
